PART II—TOOLS FOR THINKING

第二部分——思考的工具

Chapter 4 Strategies for Analytical Judgment: Transcending the Limits of Incomplete Information

第4章：分析判断的策略：超越不完整信息的限度
情报分析人员应当对自己的推理过程有所自觉。他们应当思考自己如何作出判断、得出结论，而不只是思考判断和结论本身。韦氏词典把判断定义为：在事实尚未查清时，“在迹象与概率的基础上得出决定或结论”。38 判断是分析人员填补知识空白的工具。它要求超越现有信息，是应对不确定性的主要手段，而且总是涉及一次分析上的跳跃，从已知跃入不确定。
原文

When intelligence analysts make thoughtful analytical judgments, how do they do it? In seeking answers to this question, this chapter discusses the strengths and limitations of situational logic, theory, comparison, and simple immersion in the data as strategies for the generation and evaluation of hypotheses. The final section discusses alternative strategies for choosing among hypotheses. One strategy too often used by intelligence analysts is described as “satisficing”—choosing the first hypothesis that appears good enough rather than carefully identifying all possible hypotheses and determining which is most consistent with the evidence.37

当情报分析人员作出深思熟虑的分析判断时，他们是怎么做到的？为寻求这一问题的答案，本章讨论情境逻辑、理论、比较，以及单纯沉浸于数据，作为生成和评价假设之策略各自的优势与局限。最后一节讨论在诸假设间抉择的替代策略。情报分析人员太常使用的一种策略被描述为“满意即可”——选择第一个看起来足够好的假设，而非仔细辨识所有可能的假设、并确定哪一个与证据最一致。37

* * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *

Intelligence analysts should be self-conscious about their reasoning process. They should think about how they make judgments and reach conclusions, not just about the judgments and conclusions themselves. Webster’s dictionary defines judgment as arriving at a “decision or conclusion on the basis of indications and probabilities when the facts are not clearly ascertained.” 38 Judgment is what analysts use to fill gaps in their knowledge. It entails going beyond the available information and is the principal means of coping with uncertainty. It always involves an analytical leap, from the known into the uncertain.

判断是一切情报分析不可分割的组成部分。虽然情报收集的最优目标是完备的知识，但这一目标在实践中很少达到。几乎从情报使命的定义本身来看，情报议题就涉及相当大的不确定性。因此，分析人员通常要处理不完整、模糊、且往往相互矛盾的数据。情报分析人员的功能或许可以描述为：通过运用分析判断，超越不完整信息的限度。
原文

Judgment is an integral part of all intelligence analysis. While the optimal goal of intelligence collection is complete knowledge, this goal is seldom reached in practice. Almost by definition of the intelligence mission, intelligence issues involve considerable uncertainty. Thus, the analyst is commonly working with incomplete, ambiguous, and often contradictory data. The intelligence analyst’s function might be described as transcending the limits of incomplete information through the exercise of analytical judgment.

判断的终极本质仍是一个谜。然而，我们可以识别出，分析人员在准备作出判断时会运用哪些策略来处理信息。分析策略之所以重要，是因为它们影响一个人所关注的数据。它们决定分析人员把探照灯照向何处，而这不可避免地影响分析过程的结果。
原文

The ultimate nature of judgment remains a mystery. It is possible, however, to identify diverse strategies that analysts employ to process information as they prepare to pass judgment. Analytical strategies are important because they influence the data one attends to. They determine where the analyst shines his or her searchlight, and this inevitably affects the outcome of the analytical process.

Strategies for Generating and Evaluating Hypotheses

生成和评价假设的策略
本书在最广义上使用“假设”一词，指一种有待通过收集和呈现证据来检验的潜在解释或结论。考察分析人员如何生成和评价假设，可辨识出三种主要策略——理论的运用、情境逻辑和比较——每一种都在下文有相当篇幅的讨论。一种“非策略”，即沉浸于数据并让数据自己说话，也一并讨论。这份分析策略清单并不详尽。其他策略可能包括，例如，把一个人自身的心理需求投射到手头的数据上，但本讨论不关注错误判断的病理学。相反，目标是理解那几种细致、尽责的分析——这正是人们希望并期待在一批处理高度复杂议题的情报分析人员中看到的。
原文

This book uses the term hypothesis in its broadest sense as a potential explanation or conclusion that is to be tested by collecting and presenting evidence. Examination of how analysts generate and evaluate hypotheses identifies three principal strategies—the application of theory, situational logic, and comparison—each of which is discussed at some length below. A “non-strategy,” immersion in the data and letting the data speak for themselves, is also discussed. This list of analytical strategies is not exhaustive. Other strategies might include, for example, projecting one’s own psychological needs onto the data at hand, but this discussion is not concerned with the pathology of erroneous judgment. Rather, the goal is to understand the several kinds of careful, conscientious analysis one would hope and expect to find among a cadre of intelligence analysts dealing with highly complex issues.

Situational Logic

情境逻辑
这是情报分析人员最常见的运作模式。生成和分析假设，始于考量当前情境的具体要素，而不是从涵盖许多相似案例的宽泛概括入手。该情境被视为独一无二的，因而必须依其自身独特的逻辑来理解，而不是作为一大类可比事件中的一个例子。
原文

This is the most common operating mode for intelligence analysts. Generation and analysis of hypotheses start with consideration of concrete elements of the current situation, rather than with broad generalizations that encompass many similar cases. The situation is regarded as one-of-a-kind, so that it must be understood in terms of its own unique logic, rather than as one example of a broad class of comparable events.

从当前情境的已知事实，以及对那个特定时间地点起作用的独特力量的理解出发，分析人员力图辨识这一情境在逻辑上的前因或后果。发展出一个作为可信叙事而连贯成立的情景。分析人员可以向后回溯以解释当前情境的起源或成因，或向前推演以估计未来结果。
原文

Starting with the known facts of the current situation and an understanding of the unique forces at work at that particular time and place, the analyst seeks to identify the logical antecedents or consequences of this situation. A scenario is developed that hangs together as a plausible narrative. The analyst may work backwards to explain the origins or causes of the current situation or forward to estimate the future outcome.

情境逻辑通常聚焦于追溯因果关系，或者，在处理有目的的行为时，聚焦于手段-目的关系。分析人员辨识出所追求的目标，并解释为什么外国行为者相信某些手段将实现这些目标。
原文

Situational logic commonly focuses on tracing cause-effect relationships or, when dealing with purposive behavior, means-ends relationships. The analyst identifies the goals being pursued and explains why the foreign actor(s) believe certain means will achieve those goals.

情境逻辑的特有优势在于其广泛的适用性，以及整合大量相关细节的能力。任何情境，无论多么独特，都可以用这种方式加以分析。
原文

Particular strengths of situational logic are its wide applicability and ability to integrate a large volume of relevant detail. Any situation, however unique, may be analyzed in this manner.

作为一种分析策略，情境逻辑也有两个主要弱点。其一是，理解外国领导人和政府的心理与官僚过程如此困难。要像外国领导人自己所看到的那样去看待他们所面对的选项，一个人就必须理解他们的价值观和假定，乃至他们的错误感知和误解。没有这样的洞见，诠释外国领导人的决定或预测其未来决定，往往不过是部分知情的臆测。外国的行为太频繁地显得“不理性”或“不符合他们自身的最佳利益”。这样的结论往往表明，分析人员把美国的价值观和概念框架投射到了外国领导人和社会身上，而没有按情境在他们看来的那样去理解其逻辑。
原文

Situational logic as an analytical strategy also has two principal weaknesses. One is that it is so difficult to understand the mental and bureaucratic processes of foreign leaders and governments. To see the options faced by foreign leaders as these leaders see them, one must understand their values and assumptions and even their misperceptions and misunderstandings. Without such insight, interpreting foreign leaders’ decisions or forecasting future decisions is often little more than partially informed speculation. Too frequently, foreign behavior appears “irrational” or “not in their own best interest.” Such conclusions often indicate analysts have projected American values and conceptual frameworks onto the foreign leaders and societies, rather than understanding the logic of the situation as it appears to them.

第二个弱点是，情境逻辑未能利用从研究其他国家和其他时期的相似现象中得来的理论知识。民族分离主义运动这一主题说明了这一点。民族主义是一个数百年之久的问题，但大多数西方工业民主国家一向被认为是整合良好的民族共同体。即便如此，近年来还是出现了来自寻求独立或自治的少数族群的压力上升。为什么这一现象近来出现在苏格兰、法国南部和科西嘉、魁北克、比利时部分地区和西班牙——以及在可能被预期出现它的较不稳定的第三世界国家？
原文

The second weakness is that situational logic fails to exploit the theoretical knowledge derived from study of similar phenomena in other countries and other time periods. The subject of national separatist movements illustrates the point. Nationalism is a centuries-old problem, but most Western industrial democracies have been considered well-integrated national communities. Even so, recent years have seen an increase in pressures from minority ethnic groups seeking independence or autonomy. Why has this phenomenon occurred recently in Scotland, southern France and Corsica, Quebec, parts of Belgium, and Spain—as well as in less stable Third World countries where it might be expected?

以情境逻辑模式处理这一主题，一位国别分析人员会考察该国利益攸关的各种政治、经济和社会群体。基于这些群体相对的权力地位、它们之间的动态互动，以及可能影响利益相关方未来地位的预期趋势或发展，分析人员会力图辨识将决定最终结果的驱动力量。
原文

Dealing with this topic in a logic-of-the-situation mode, a country analyst would examine the diverse political, economic, and social groups whose interests are at stake in the country. Based on the relative power positions of these groups, the dynamic interactions among them, and anticipated trends or developments that might affect the future positions of the interested parties, the analyst would seek to identify the driving forces that will determine the eventual outcome.

完全有可能以这种方式写出一份关于某单一国家分离主义运动的详尽、看似消息灵通的研究，却忽略这样一个事实：族群冲突作为一种一般性现象，已成为大量理论研究的主题。通过研究许多国家中的相似现象，一个人可以生成并评价关于根本成因的假设，而只处理单一情境逻辑的分析人员，甚至可能根本想不到这些根本成因。例如，长期蛰伏的族群情感的复苏，在多大程度上源自对现代大众传播系统所伴随的文化同质化的一种反应？
原文

It is quite possible to write in this manner a detailed and seemingly well-informed study of a separatist movement in a single country while ignoring the fact that ethnic conflict as a generic phenomenon has been the subject of considerable theoretical study. By studying similar phenomena in many countries, one can generate and evaluate hypotheses concerning root causes that may not even be considered by an analyst who is dealing only with the logic of a single situation. For example, to what extent does the resurgence of long-dormant ethnic sentiments stem from a reaction against the cultural homogenization that accompanies modern mass communications systems?

如下文所讨论的，与情境逻辑分析中通常所考虑的相比，分析同一现象的许多例子，能使人探究得更深入，触及更根本的成因。从理论分析更宽广的视角看，情境逻辑所辨识出的近因，显得不过是表明存在更根本的因果因素的症状。更好地理解这些根本成因，对于有效的预测——尤其是较长期的预测——至关重要。虽然情境逻辑可能是估计短期发展的最佳途径，但随着分析视角进一步伸向未来，就需要一种更偏理论的途径。
原文

Analyzing many examples of a similar phenomenon, as discussed below, enables one to probe more fundamental causes than those normally considered in logic-of-the-situation analysis. The proximate causes identified by situational logic appear, from the broader perspective of theoretical analysis, to be but symptoms indicating the presence of more fundamental causal factors. A better understanding of these fundamental causes is critical to effective forecasting, especially over the longer range. While situational logic may be the best approach to estimating shortterm developments, a more theoretical approach is required as the analytical perspective moves further into the future.

Applying Theory

运用理论
理论是一个学术术语，在情报界并不十分流行，但在任何关于分析判断的讨论中都无法回避。在该术语的一种通俗含义中，“理论的”与“不切实际”和“脱离现实”这两个词相关联。不用说，此处使用的是一种颇为不同的含义。
原文

Theory is an academic term not much in vogue in the Intelligence Community, but it is unavoidable in any discussion of analytical judgment. In one popular meaning of the term, “theoretical” is associated with the terms “impractical” and “unrealistic”. Needless to say, it is used here in a quite different sense.

理论是通过研究某种现象的许多实例而得出的概括。它规定，当某一组特定条件出现时，某些其他条件将会随之发生，或是确定无疑地发生，或是以某种概率发生。换句话说，结论被判定为源自一组条件，以及一项认定这些条件适用于所分析的具体案例的判断。例如，土耳其是一个处于不稳定战略地位的发展中国家。这界定了一组条件，这些条件蕴含着关于该国军队所扮演的角色以及政治进程性质的结论，因为分析人员对这些因素通常如何相互关联有一种即便不是明确的、也是隐含的理解。
原文

A theory is a generalization based on the study of many examples of some phenomenon. It specifies that when a given set of conditions arises, certain other conditions will follow either with certainty or with some degree of probability. In other words, conclusions are judged to follow from a set of conditions and a finding that these conditions apply in the specific case being analyzed. For example, Turkey is a developing country in a precarious strategic position. This defines a set of conditions that imply conclusions concerning the role of the military and the nature of political processes in that country, because analysts have an implicit if not explicit understanding of how these factors normally relate.

学者们所称的理论，实际上只不过是对情报分析人员本就具备的一种基本理解——即他们对个人、机构和政治系统通常如何行事的理解——所作的更为明确的表述而已。
原文

What academics refer to as theory is really only a more explicit version of what intelligence analysts think of as their basic understanding of how individuals, institutions, and political systems normally behave.

在情报分析中运用理论既有优点也有缺点。一个优点是“理论节省思考”。通过识别一个问题的关键要素，理论使分析人员能够梳理大量不那么重要的细节。理论使分析人员能够看到今日短暂事态发展之外的东西，能够辨认哪些趋势是表面的、哪些是重要的，并能够预见目前几乎没有具体证据的未来事态发展。
原文

There are both advantages and drawbacks to applying theory in intelligence analysis. One advantage is that “theory economizes thought.” By identifying the key elements of a problem, theory enables an analyst to sort through a mass of less significant detail. Theory enables the analyst to see beyond today’s transient developments, to recognize which trends are superficial and which are significant, and to foresee future developments for which there is today little concrete evidence.

例如，考虑这样一个理论命题：在一个封建社会中，经济发展和大量涌入的外来思想会导致政治不稳定。这一命题似乎相当站得住脚。当运用于沙特阿拉伯时，它暗示沙特君主制的时日已经屈指可数，尽管使用情境逻辑的沙特局势分析人员发现，目前几乎没有或根本没有证据表明王室的权力和地位受到实质性威胁。因此，运用一个普遍接受的理论命题使分析人员能够预测一种“确凿证据”尚未开始形成的结果。当运用于现实世界问题时，这是理论分析的一个重要优势。
原文

Consider, for example, the theoretical proposition that economic development and massive infusion of foreign ideas in a feudal society lead to political instability. This proposition seems well established. When applied to Saudi Arabia, it suggests that the days of the Saudi monarchy are numbered, although analysts of the Saudi scene using situational logic find little or no current evidence of a meaningful threat to the power and position of the royal family. Thus, the application of a generally accepted theoretical proposition enables the analyst to forecast an outcome for which the “hard evidence” has not yet begun to develop. This is an important strength of theoretical analysis when applied to realworld problems.

然而，这同一个例子也说明了将理论运用于政治现象分析中的一个常见弱点。理论命题往往未能明确事态发展可能被预期发生的时间框架。就沙特阿拉伯而言，分析上的问题与其说是君主制最终是否会被取代，不如说是这可能在何时或在何种条件下发生。对将封建社会中经济发展和外来思想与政治不稳定联系起来的理论作进一步阐述，就能够识别出分析人员可能寻找的早期预警指标。这类指标将既指导情报收集，也指导对社会政治和社会经济数据的分析，并引出关于此类事件可能在何时或在何种情况下发生的假设。
原文

Yet this same example also illustrates a common weakness in applying theory to analysis of political phenomena. Theoretical propositions frequently fail to specify the time frame within which developments might be anticipated to occur. The analytical problem with respect to Saudi Arabia is not so much whether the monarchy will eventually be replaced, as when or under what conditions this might happen. Further elaboration of the theory relating economic development and foreign ideas to political instability in feudal societies would identify early warning indicators that analysts might look for. Such indicators would guide both intelligence collection and analysis of sociopolitical and socioeconomic data and lead to hypotheses concerning when or under what circumstances such an event might occur.

但是，如果说理论使分析人员能够超越可用数据的局限，那么它也可能成为忽视真正预示未来事件的证据的依据。结合20世纪70年代末针对伊朗沙阿的民众骚动，考虑以下这些理论命题：（1）当一个威权统治者的地位受到威胁时，他必要时会用武力捍卫自己的地位。（2）一个享有有效的军队和安全部队完全支持的威权统治者，不可能被民意和骚动所推翻。很少有人会质疑这些命题，然而当将它们运用于20世纪70年代末的伊朗时，它们导致伊朗问题专家误判了沙阿保住孔雀宝座的机会。许多——即便不是大多数——这样的专家似乎确信沙阿依然强势，而且当异见威胁到失控时他会加以镇压。在事后看来似乎是相反的有力证据积累起来之后的好几个月里，许多人仍然坚持这一评估。
原文

But if theory enables the analyst to transcend the limits of available data, it may also provide the basis for ignoring evidence that is truly indicative of future events. Consider the following theoretical propositions in the light of popular agitation against the Shah of Iran in the late 1970s: (1) When the position of an authoritarian ruler is threatened, he will defend his position with force if necessary. (2) An authoritarian ruler enjoying complete support of effective military and security forces cannot be overthrown by popular opinion and agitation. Few would challenge these propositions, yet when applied to Iran in the late 1970s, they led Iran specialists to misjudge the Shah’s chances for retaining the peacock throne. Many if not most such specialists seemed convinced that the Shah remained strong and that he would crack down on dissent when it threatened to get out of control. Many persisted in this assessment for several months after the accumulation of what in retrospect appears to have been strong evidence to the contrary.

这些假定的持续存在，用心理学术语很容易理解。当证据缺乏或含糊不清时，分析人员通过运用自己关于政治系统和行为本质的一般背景知识来评估假设。关于沙阿的实力以及他镇压异见者意图的证据是含糊不清的，但这位伊朗君主是一个威权统治者，而威权政权被假定具有某些特征，正如前面所引述的命题中所指出的那样。因此，关于沙阿的信念嵌入于关于威权政权本身性质的广泛而有说服力的假定之中。对于一个相信上述两个命题的分析人员来说，要推断出沙阿将被推翻，所需要的证据——包括更为明确的证据——要远远多于为继续对其前途保持信心所需要的证据。39
原文

Persistence of these assumptions is easily understood in psychological terms. When evidence is lacking or ambiguous, the analyst evaluates hypotheses by applying his or her general background knowledge concerning the nature of political systems and behavior. The evidence on the strength of the Shah and his intention to crack down on dissidents was ambiguous, but the Iranian monarch was an authoritarian ruler, and authoritarian regimes were assumed to have certain characteristics, as noted in the previously cited propositions. Thus beliefs about the Shah were embedded in broad and persuasive assumptions about the nature of authoritarian regimes per se. For an analyst who believed in the two aforementioned propositions, it would have taken far more evidence, including more unambiguous evidence, to infer that the Shah would be overthrown than to justify continued confidence in his future.39

然而，理论与情境逻辑之间的区别并不像从这张图表看上去的那样清晰。情境逻辑分析也大量依赖于理论假定。如果没有某种将某些结果的可能性与某些先决条件联系起来的隐含理论，分析人员如何选择最重要的要素来描述当前局势，或识别这些要素的起因或后果呢？
原文

The distinction between theory and situational logic is not as clear as it may seem from this graphic, however. Logic-of-the-situation analysis also draws heavily on theoretical assumptions. How does the analyst select the most significant elements to describe the current situation, or identify the causes or consequences of these elements, without some implicit theory that relates the likelihood of certain outcomes to certain antecedent conditions?

例如，如果估计一场即将到来的选举结果的分析人员没有当前的民调数据，就有必要回顾过去的选举，研究竞选活动，然后判断选民可能会对当前的竞选活动以及影响选民态度的事件作出何种反应。在这样做时，分析人员是从一组关于人性以及什么驱动个人和群体的假定出发的。这些假定构成了一种政治行为理论的一部分，但它是一种不同于理论分析下所讨论的理论。它并不阐明整个局势，而只是阐明局势中的一小部分，而且它可能不适用于所关注的具体国家之外。此外，它更有可能保持隐含状态，而不是成为分析的焦点。
原文

For example, if the analyst estimating the outcome of an impending election does not have current polling data, it is necessary to look back at past elections, study the campaigns, and then judge how voters are likely to react to the current campaigns and to events that influence voter attitudes. In doing so, the analyst operates from a set of assumptions about human nature and what drives people and groups. These assumptions form part of a theory of political behavior, but it is a different sort of theory than was discussed under theoretical analysis. It does not illuminate the entire situation, but only a small increment of the situation, and it may not apply beyond the specific country of concern. Further, it is much more likely to remain implicit, rather than be a focal point of the analysis.

Comparison with Historical Situations

与历史情境的比较
超越可用信息的第三种方法是比较。分析人员通过将当前事件与同一国家的历史先例相比较，或与其他国家的类似事件相比较，来寻求对当前事件的理解。类比是比较的一种形式。当一个历史情境被认为与当前情况相类似时，分析人员利用他们对历史先例的理解来填补他们对当前局势理解上的空缺。当前的未知要素被假定与历史先例的已知要素相同。因此，分析人员推断，同样的力量在起作用，当前局势的结果很可能与历史情境的结果相似，或者需要某种政策才能避免出现与过去相同的结果。
原文

A third approach for going beyond the available information is comparison. An analyst seeks understanding of current events by comparing them with historical precedents in the same country, or with similar events in other countries. Analogy is one form of comparison. When an historical situation is deemed comparable to current circumstances, analysts use their understanding of the historical precedent to fill gaps in their understanding of the current situation. Unknown elements of the present are assumed to be the same as known elements of the historical precedent. Thus, analysts reason that the same forces are at work, that the outcome of the present situation is likely to be similar to the outcome of the historical situation, or that a certain policy is required in order to avoid the same outcome as in the past.

比较不同于情境逻辑之处在于，当前局势是结合一个或多或少明确的概念模型来解读的，而这个模型是通过考察其他时间或地点的类似情境创建出来的。它不同于理论分析之处在于，这个概念模型基于单个案例或仅仅少数几个案例，而不是基于许多类似案例。比较也可用于产生理论，但这是一种更为狭隘的理论构建，其有效性远不如从许多可比案例中推断出的概括那样能够得到验证。
原文

Comparison differs from situational logic in that the present situation is interpreted in the light of a more or less explicit conceptual model that is created by looking at similar situations in other times or places. It differs from theoretical analysis in that this conceptual model is based on a single case or only a few cases, rather than on many similar cases. Comparison may also be used to generate theory, but this is a more narrow kind of theorizing that cannot be validated nearly as well as generalizations inferred from many comparable cases.

通过比较进行推理是一条便捷的捷径，当其他分析策略既无数据也无理论可用时，或者仅仅因为它比更详尽的分析更容易且更省时，人们就会选择它。一次审慎的比较分析首先要明确当前局势的关键要素。分析人员随后寻找一个或多个可能对当前有所启示的历史先例。然而，一个历史先例常常可能如此生动而有力，以至于从一开始就把自己强加于一个人的思维之中，使他们习惯于主要根据当前与过去的相似性来感知当前。这就是通过类比进行推理。正如罗伯特·杰维斯所指出的，“历史类比往往先于、而非后于对一种情境的审慎分析。”40
原文

Reasoning by comparison is a convenient shortcut, one chosen when neither data nor theory are available for the other analytical strategies, or simply because it is easier and less time-consuming than a more detailed analysis. A careful comparative analysis starts by specifying key elements of the present situation. The analyst then seeks out one or more historical precedents that may shed light on the present. Frequently, however, a historical precedent may be so vivid and powerful that it imposes itself upon a person’s thinking from the outset, conditioning them to perceive the present primarily in terms of its similarity to the past. This is reasoning by analogy. As Robert Jervis noted, “historical analogies often precede, rather than follow, a careful analysis of a situation.”40

把当代事件与早先事件联系起来作为理解指南的倾向是一种强大的倾向。比较通过将陌生的事物化约为熟悉的事物来帮助达成理解。在缺乏全面理解当前局势所需数据的情况下，通过比较进行推理可能是唯一的选择。然而，任何采取这种方法的人都应当意识到出错的巨大可能性。这种做法是对以下两点的一种隐含承认：缺乏充分的信息来就当前局势本身进行理解，以及缺乏相关的理论来将当前局势与许多其他可比情境联系起来。
原文

The tendency to relate contemporary events to earlier events as a guide to understanding is a powerful one. Comparison helps achieve understanding by reducing the unfamiliar to the familiar. In the absence of data required for a full understanding of the current situation, reasoning by comparison may be the only alternative. Anyone taking this approach, however, should be aware of the significant potential for error. This course is an implicit admission of the lack of sufficient information to understand the present situation in its own right, and lack of relevant theory to relate the present situation to many other comparable situations

当然，困难在于确信两种情境是否真正可比。因为它们在某些方面是对等的，就有一种倾向，即仿佛它们在所有方面都对等那样进行推理，并假定当前局势将会有与历史情境相同或相似的结果。只有当这一假定建立在对当前局势和历史先例两者的深入分析之上、以确保它们在所有相关方面确实可比时，它才是一个有效的假定。
原文

The difficulty, of course, is in being certain that two situations are truly comparable. Because they are equivalent in some respects, there is a tendency to reason as though they were equivalent in all respects, and to assume that the current situation will have the same or similar outcome as the historical situation. This is a valid assumption only when based on in-depth analysis of both the current situation and the historical precedent to ensure that they are actually comparable in all relevant respects.

在一本所有情报分析人员都应当熟悉的短篇著作中，欧内斯特·梅追溯了历史类比对美国外交政策的影响。41 他发现，由于通过类比进行推理，美国政策制定者往往落后一代人，决心避免上一代人的错误。他们推行的政策本来会最适合于历史情境，但未必很好地适应当前局势。
原文

In a short book that ought to be familiar to all intelligence analysts, Ernest May traced the impact of historical analogy on US foreign policy.41 He found that because of reasoning by analogy, US policymakers tend to be one generation behind, determined to avoid the mistakes of the previous generation. They pursue the policies that would have been most appropriate in the historical situation but are not necessarily well adapted to the current one.

例如，20世纪30年代的政策制定者将国际局势视为类似于第一次世界大战之前的局势。因此，他们奉行一种孤立政策，这种政策本来适合于防止美国卷入第一次世界大战，却未能阻止第二次世界大战。第二次世界大战之后的共产主义侵略被视为类似于纳粹侵略，从而导致了一种遏制政策，而这种政策本来是能够阻止第二次世界大战的。
原文

Policymakers in the 1930s, for instance, viewed the international situation as analogous to that before World War I. Consequently, they followed a policy of isolation that would have been appropriate for preventing American involvement in the first World War but failed to prevent the second. Communist aggression after World War II was seen as analogous to Nazi aggression, leading to a policy of containment that could have prevented World War II.

更近一些，越南类比多年来被反复用于反对一种积极干预的美国外交政策。例如，一些人用越南类比来反对美国参与海湾战争——这是一个有缺陷的类比，因为在科威特/伊拉克进行战斗的作战地形与越南完全不同，而且在那里的地形与越南相比对我们有利得多。
原文

More recently, the Vietnam analogy has been used repeatedly over many years to argue against an activist US foreign policy. For example, some used the Vietnam analogy to argue against US participation in the Gulf War—a flawed analogy because the operating terrain over which battles were fought was completely different in Kuwait/Iraq and much more in our favor there as compared with Vietnam.

梅认为，政策制定者往往用与过去类比的方式来看待问题，但他们通常糟糕地运用历史：
原文

May argues that policymakers often perceive problems in terms of analogies with the past, but that they ordinarily use history badly:

当求助于类比时，他们往往抓住最先想到的那一个。他们不作更广泛的研究。他们也不停下来分析这个案例、检验它的适用性，甚至不问一问它可能在哪些方面具有误导性。42
原文
When resorting to an analogy, they tend to seize upon the first that comes to mind. They do not research more widely. Nor do they pause to analyze the case, test its fitness, or even ask in what ways it might be misleading.42
与政策制定者相比，情报分析人员有更多的可用时间来“分析而非类比”。情报分析人员往往是优秀的历史学家，有大量可供回忆的历史先例。一个分析人员可供支配的潜在类比对象数量越多，选中一个恰当类比的可能性就越大。一个分析人员知识的深度越深，他就越有可能察觉到两种情境之间的差异以及相似之处。然而，即便在最好的情况下，基于与单个类似情境相比较的推断，也可能比大多数其他形式的推断更容易出错。
原文

As compared with policymakers, intelligence analysts have more time available to “analyze rather than analogize.” Intelligence analysts tend to be good historians, with a large number of historical precedents available for recall. The greater the number of potential analogues an analyst has at his or her disposal, the greater the likelihood of selecting an appropriate one. The greater the depth of an analyst’s knowledge, the greater the chances the analyst will perceive the differences as well as the similarities between two situations. Even under the best of circumstances, however, inferences based on comparison with a single analogous situation probably are more prone to error than most other forms of inference.

比较分析最富有成效的用途是提出假设和凸显差异，而不是得出结论。比较能够暗示当前局势中并不容易显现的变量的存在或影响，或者能够激发想象力去构想分析人员本来不会想到的解释或可能结果。简而言之，比较能够产生假设，这些假设随后指导对额外信息的搜寻，以证实或反驳这些假设。然而，除非对两种情境都作了透彻分析、确认它们确实可比，否则比较不应构成结论的基础。
原文

The most productive uses of comparative analysis are to suggest hypotheses and to highlight differences, not to draw conclusions. Comparison can suggest the presence or the influence of variables that are not readily apparent in the current situation, or stimulate the imagination to conceive explanations or possible outcomes that might not otherwise occur to the analyst. In short, comparison can generate hypotheses that then guide the search for additional information to confirm or refute these hypotheses. It should not, however, form the basis for conclusions unless thorough analysis of both situations has confirmed they are indeed comparable.

Data Immersion

沉浸于数据
分析人员有时把他们的工作程序描述为让自己沉浸于数据之中，而不把数据套入任何先入为主的模式。在某个时刻，一个看似成立的模式（或答案或解释）自发地浮现出来，分析人员随后回到数据，检验数据在多大程度上支持这一判断。按照这种观点，客观性要求分析人员抑制任何个人意见或先入之见，从而只受案例“事实”的引导。
原文

Analysts sometimes describe their work procedure as immersing themselves in the data without fitting the data into any preconceived pattern. At some point an apparent pattern (or answer or explanation) emerges spontaneously, and the analyst then goes back to the data to check how well the data support this judgment. According to this view, objectivity requires the analyst to suppress any personal opinions or preconceptions, so as to be guided only by the “facts” of the case.

以这种方式看待分析，忽视了这样一个事实：信息不能为自己说话。信息的意义始终是信息的性质与解读它的语境两者共同作用的产物。语境由分析人员以一组关于人类和组织行为的假定和预期的形式提供。这些先入之见是决定哪些信息被视为相关、以及如何对其进行解读的关键因素。
原文

To think of analysis in this way overlooks the fact that information cannot speak for itself. The significance of information is always a joint function of the nature of the information and the context in which it is interpreted. The context is provided by the analyst in the form of a set of assumptions and expectations concerning human and organizational behavior. These preconceptions are critical determinants of which information is considered relevant and how it is interpreted.

当然，在许多情况下，分析人员别无选择，只能让自己沉浸于数据之中。显然，分析人员在开始分析之前必须有一个可供工作的知识基础。当处理一个新的、不熟悉的主题时，不加批判地、相对不加选择地积累和查阅信息是一个恰当的第一步。但这是一个吸收信息的过程，而不是分析信息的过程。
原文

Of course there are many circumstances in which the analyst has no option but to immerse himself or herself in the data. Obviously, an analyst must have a base of knowledge to work with before starting analysis. When dealing with a new and unfamiliar subject, the uncritical and relatively non-selective accumulation and review of information is an appropriate first step. But this is a process of absorbing information, not analyzing it.

分析始于分析人员有意识地将自己插入到过程中，去选择、分类和组织信息。这种选择和组织只能依据有意识或潜意识的假定和先入之见来完成。
原文

Analysis begins when the analyst consciously inserts himself or herself into the process to select, sort, and organize information. This selection and organization can only be accomplished according to conscious or subconscious assumptions and preconceptions.

问题不在于一个人先前的假定和预期是否会影响分析，而只在于这种影响是被明确表述出来还是保持隐含状态。这一区别看来是重要的。在一项旨在确定医生如何作出医学诊断的研究中，作为受试者的医生被要求描述他们的分析策略。那些强调透彻收集数据作为其主要分析方法的医生，其诊断的准确性显著低于那些自称遵循其他分析策略（例如识别和检验假设）的医生。43 此外，通过在病史采集和体格检查中更加透彻而收集额外数据，并没有带来诊断准确性的提高。44
原文

The question is not whether one’s prior assumptions and expectations influence analysis, but only whether this influence is made explicit or remains implicit. The distinction appears to be important. In research to determine how physicians make medical diagnoses, the doctors who comprised the test subjects were asked to describe their analytical strategies. Those who stressed thorough collection of data as their principal analytical method were significantly less accurate in their diagnoses than those who described themselves as following other analytical strategies such as identifying and testing hypotheses.43 Moreover, the collection of additional data through greater thoroughness in the medical history and physical examination did not lead to increased diagnostic accuracy.44

人们或许可以推测，有些分析人员为了追求更大的客观性，会压制自己对主观投入的认知——但这样做，他们实际能作出的有效投入反而更少。客观性是通过将假定明确表述出来、以便可以对其加以检验和质疑而获得的，而不是通过徒劳地努力将它们从分析中消除而获得的。
原文

One might speculate that the analyst who seeks greater objectivity by suppressing recognition of his or her own subjective input actually has less valid input to make. Objectivity is gained by making assumptions explicit so that they may be examined and challenged, not by vain efforts to eliminate them from analysis.

Relationships Among Strategies

各策略之间的关系
没有哪一种策略必然比其他策略更好。为了生成所有相关的假设，并最大限度地利用所有可能相关的信息，在研究项目早期的假设生成阶段采用全部三种策略是可取的做法。不幸的是，分析人员通常缺乏这样做的意愿或时间。
原文

No one strategy is necessarily better than the others. In order to generate all relevant hypotheses and make maximum use of all potentially relevant information, it would be desirable to employ all three strategies at the early hypothesis generation phase of a research project. Unfortunately, analysts commonly lack the inclination or time to do so.

不同的分析人员有着不同的分析习惯和对分析策略的偏好。作为一个允许存在大量例外的宽泛概括，接受过地区研究或历史学训练的分析人员往往偏好情境逻辑，而具有深厚社会科学背景的人则更有可能把理论洞见和比较洞见运用到工作中。情报界作为一个整体，在情境逻辑方面远比在理论方面更强。依我之见，情报分析人员的概括不够，这与许多概括过度的学术学者恰恰相反。在政治分析中尤其如此，而这并不完全是由于缺乏可适用的政治理论。那些现有的理论洞见，往往不为政治情报分析人员所知，或者至少未被他们所使用。
原文

Different analysts have different analytical habits and preferences for analytical strategy. As a broad generalization that admits numerous exceptions, analysts trained in area studies or history tend to prefer situational logic, while those with a strong social science background are more likely to bring theoretical and comparative insights to bear on their work. The Intelligence Community as a whole is far stronger in situational logic than in theory. In my judgment, intelligence analysts do not generalize enough, as opposed to many academic scholars who generalize too much. This is especially true in political analysis, and it is not entirely due to unavailability of applicable political theory. Theoretical insights that are available are often unknown to or at least not used by political intelligence analysts.

分析策略上的差异，可能会在情报分析人员与他们为其撰稿的某些政策制定者之间造成根本性的视角差异。那些并非相关议题专家的高层官员，运用的理论和比较远多于情报分析人员，而运用的情境逻辑则远少于后者。任何政策制定者或其他高级管理人员，若缺乏专家的知识基础，又没有时间钻研细节，就必然要处理宽泛的概括。有许多决策需要作出，而用于考虑其中每一项决策的时间，远少于情报分析人员所拥有的时间。这就要求政策制定者采取更为概念化的方法，用能够概括大量细节的理论、模型或类比来思考。这究竟代表着老练还是过度简化，取决于具体的个案，而且或许还取决于人们是否认同所作出的判断。无论如何，情报分析人员在为其消费者撰稿时，最好把这一现象考虑在内。
原文

Differences in analytical strategy may cause fundamental differences in perspective between intelligence analysts and some of the policymakers for whom they write. Higher level officials who are not experts on the subject at issue use far more theory and comparison and less situational logic than intelligence analysts. Any policymaker or other senior manager who lacks the knowledge base of the specialist and does not have time for detail must, of necessity, deal with broad generalizations. Many decisions must be made, with much less time to consider each of them than is available to the intelligence analyst. This requires the policymaker to take a more conceptual approach, to think in terms of theories, models, or analogies that summarize large amounts of detail. Whether this represents sophistication or oversimplification depends upon the individual case and, perhaps, whether one agrees or disagrees with the judgments made. In any event, intelligence analysts would do well to take this phenomenon into account when writing for their consumers.

Strategies for Choice Among Hypotheses

在各假设之间进行选择的策略
一个系统化的分析过程要求在各种备选假设之间进行选择，而正是在这里，分析实践往往与理想状态、与科学方法的准则显著背离。理想的做法是生成一整套完整的假设，系统地评估每一个假设，然后找出与数据最为吻合的那个假设。至于科学方法，它要求人们力求证伪假设，而非证实假设。
原文

A systematic analytical process requires selection among alternative hypotheses, and it is here that analytical practice often diverges significantly from the ideal and from the canons of scientific method. The ideal is to generate a full set of hypotheses, systematically evaluate each hypothesis, and then identify the hypothesis that provides the best fit to the data. Scientific method, for its part, requires that one seek to disprove hypotheses rather than confirm them.

在实践中，人们通常采用其他策略。亚历山大·乔治指出了若干种在面对不完整信息以及多重、相互竞争的价值观和目标时用于作出决策的非最优策略。虽然乔治所构想的这些策略适用于决策者如何在各种备选政策之间进行选择，但其中大多数也适用于情报分析人员可能如何在各种备选分析假设之间作出抉择。
原文

In practice, other strategies are commonly employed. Alexander George has identified a number of less-than-optimal strategies for making decisions in the face of incomplete information and multiple, competing values and goals. While George conceived of these strategies as applicable to how decisionmakers choose among alternative policies, most also apply to how intelligence analysts might decide among alternative analytical hypotheses.

乔治所指出的相关策略是：
原文

The relevant strategies George identified are:

  • • "Satisficing"—selecting the first identified alternative that appears "good enough" rather than examining all alternatives to determine which is "best."
    “满意即可”——选择第一个看起来“足够好”的已识别备选方案，而不是逐一考察所有备选方案以确定哪一个“最好”。
  • • Incrementalism—focusing on a narrow range of alternatives representing marginal change, without considering the need for dramatic change from an existing position.
    渐进主义——聚焦于代表边际变化的狭窄一组备选方案，而不考虑从现有立场作出重大变革的必要性。
  • • Consensus—opting for the alternative that will elicit the greatest agreement and support. Simply telling the boss what he or she wants to hear is one version of this.
    求取共识——选择那个能够赢得最广泛认同和支持的备选方案。仅仅告诉上司他或她想听的话，就是这种做法的一种表现形式。
  • • Reasoning by analogy—choosing the alternative that appears most likely to avoid some previous error or to duplicate a previous success.
    类比推理——选择那个看起来最有可能避免某个先前错误、或复制某个先前成功的备选方案。
  • • Relying on a set of principles or maxims that distinguish a "good" from a "bad" alternative.45
    依靠一套用以区分“好”备选方案与“坏”备选方案的原则或准则。45
    • 情报分析人员还有一个政策制定者所不具备的诱人选项：回避作出判断，只是描述当前的态势、列出各种备选方案，然后让情报消费者去判断哪一个备选方案最有可能。这些策略中的大多数在此不作讨论。以下各段仅聚焦于在情报分析中看来最为普遍的那一种。
    原文

    The intelligence analyst has another tempting option not available to the policymaker: to avoid judgment by simply describing the current situation, identifying alternatives, and letting the intelligence consumer make the judgment about which alternative is most likely. Most of these strategies are not discussed here. The following paragraphs focus only on the one that seems most prevalent in intelligence analysis.

    “Satisficing”

    “满意即可”
    基于个人经验以及与分析人员的讨论，我倾向于认为，大多数分析都是以一种与“满意即可”模式（选择第一个看起来“足够好”的已识别备选方案）极为相似的方式进行的。46 分析人员识别出看来最有可能的那个假设——也就是那个看来最为准确的、对态势的初步估计、解释或描述。数据的收集和整理，取决于它们是否支持这一初步判断，而如果这个假设看来与数据相当吻合，它就会被接受。谨慎的分析人员随后会对其他可能的假设、以及未被首选判断所解释的证据作一番快速的复查，以确保自己没有忽略某项重要的考虑因素。
    原文

    I would suggest, based on personal experience and discussions with analysts, that most analysis is conducted in a manner very similar to the satisficing mode (selecting the first identified alternative that appears “good enough”).46 The analyst identifies what appears to be the most likely hypothesis—that is, the tentative estimate, explanation, or description of the situation that appears most accurate. Data are collected and organized according to whether they support this tentative judgment, and the hypothesis is accepted if it seems to provide a reasonable fit to the data. The careful analyst will then make a quick review of other possible hypotheses and of evidence not accounted for by the preferred judgment to ensure that he or she has not overlooked some important consideration.

    这种方法有三个弱点：聚焦于单一假设所导致的选择性感知、未能生成一整套完整的竞争性假设，以及聚焦于证实而非证伪假设的证据。下面将逐一讨论这几点。
    原文

    This approach has three weaknesses: the selective perception that results from focus on a single hypothesis, failure to generate a complete set of competing hypotheses, and a focus on evidence that confirms rather than disconfirms hypotheses. Each of these is discussed below.

    选择性感知。 初步假设发挥着一种有用的功能，帮助分析人员选择、整理和管理信息。它们缩小了问题的范围，使分析人员能够高效地聚焦于最为相关和重要的数据。这些假设在工作记忆中充当着组织框架，因而有助于从记忆中检索信息。简而言之，它们是分析过程中不可或缺的要素。但它们的功能性效用也带来了一定的代价，因为假设起着感知过滤器的作用。分析人员，如同一般人一样，往往只看见他们正在寻找的东西，而忽略那些并未明确纳入其搜索策略之中的东西。他们往往把所处理的信息局限于那些与当前假设相关的信息。
    原文

    Selective Perception. Tentative hypotheses serve a useful function in helping analysts select, organize, and manage information. They narrow the scope of the problem so that the analyst can focus efficiently on data that are most relevant and important. The hypotheses serve as organizing frameworks in working memory and thus facilitate retrieval of information from memory. In short, they are essential elements of the analytical process. But their functional utility also entails some cost, because a hypothesis functions as a perceptual filter. Analysts, like people in general, tend to see what they are looking for and to overlook that which is not specifically included in their search strategy. They tend to limit the processed information to that which is relevant to the current hypothesis.

    如果这个假设是错误的，那些本可以提示一个新的或经过修正的假设的信息，就可能会被遗漏。
    原文

    If the hypothesis is incorrect, information may be lost that would suggest a new or modified hypothesis.

    这一困难可以通过同时考虑多个假设来克服。这种方法将在第8章中详细讨论。它的优点在于，把注意力集中到那少数几项在区分各竞争性假设的成立与否方面具有最大诊断价值的证据上。大多数证据都与好几种不同的假设相符，而当分析人员一次只聚焦于一个假设时——尤其是当他们的关注点在于力求证实而非证伪那个看来最有可能的答案时——这一事实很容易被忽略。
    原文

    This difficulty can be overcome by the simultaneous consideration of multiple hypotheses. This approach is discussed in detail in Chapter 8. It has the advantage of focusing attention on those few items of evidence that have the greatest diagnostic value in distinguishing among the validity of competing hypotheses. Most evidence is consistent with several different hypotheses, and this fact is easily overlooked when analysts focus on only one hypothesis at a time—especially if their focus is on seeking to confirm rather than disprove what appears to be the most likely answer.

    未能生成适当的假设。 如果初步假设决定了搜索信息以及判断其相关性的标准，那么由此可以推知，如果正确答案未被所考虑的那几个假设所涵盖，人们就可能会将其忽略。有关假设生成的研究表明，人们在这项任务上的表现糟糕得可怜。47 当面对一个分析问题时，人们要么无法、要么干脆不肯花时间去识别全部可能答案的完整范围。如果对分析过程的这一阶段给予更为审慎的关注，分析绩效或许能够得到显著提升。分析人员需要花更多时间，运用前面讨论过的全部三种策略——理论、情境逻辑和比较——来形成一整套完整的竞争性假设。
    原文

    Failure To Generate Appropriate Hypotheses. If tentative hypotheses determine the criteria for searching for information and judging its relevance, it follows that one may overlook the proper answer if it is not encompassed within the several hypotheses being considered. Research on hypothesis generation suggests that performance on this task is woefully inadequate.47 When faced with an analytical problem, people are either unable or simply do not take the time to identify the full range of potential answers. Analytical performance might be significantly enhanced by more deliberate attention to this stage of the analytical process. Analysts need to take more time to develop a full set of competing hypotheses, using all three of the previously discussed strategies—theory, situational logic, and comparison.

    未能考虑证据的诊断性。 在缺乏一整套完整的备选假设的情况下，是不可能评估证据的“诊断性”的。不幸的是，许多分析人员对证据诊断性这一概念并不熟悉。它指的是任何一项证据在多大程度上有助于分析人员确定各备选假设的相对可能性。
    原文

    Failure To Consider Diagnosticity of Evidence. In the absence of a complete set of alternative hypotheses, it is not possible to evaluate the “diagnosticity” of evidence. Unfortunately, many analysts are unfamiliar with the concept of diagnosticity of evidence. It refers to the extent to which any item of evidence helps the analyst determine the relative likelihood of alternative hypotheses.

    举例来说，高体温在告知医生病人生病了这一点上可能极有价值，但在确定病人所患的是哪一种疾病方面则价值相对较小。由于高体温与关于病人病情的如此多种可能假设都相符，因此它在确定哪一种疾病（假设）更有可能这一点上，诊断价值有限。
    原文

    To illustrate, a high temperature may have great value in telling a doctor that a patient is sick, but relatively little value in determining which illness the patient is suffering from. Because a high temperature is consistent with so many possible hypotheses about a patient’s illness, it has limited diagnostic value in determining which illness (hypothesis) is the more likely one.

    当证据影响到分析人员对各种假设相对可能性的判断时，它就具有诊断性。如果一项证据看来与所有假设都相符，那么它可能根本不具有诊断价值。人们常有这样的体验：发现大多数现有证据其实并没有多大帮助，因为它可以与所有假设相调和。
    原文

    Evidence is diagnostic when it influences an analyst’s judgment on the relative likelihood of the various hypotheses. If an item of evidence seems consistent with all the hypotheses, it may have no diagnostic value at all. It is a common experience to discover that most available evidence really is not very helpful, as it can be reconciled with all the hypotheses.

    Failure To Reject Hypotheses

    未能否定假设
    科学方法建立在否定假设这一原则之上，同时只是暂时地接受那些无法被驳倒的假设。相比之下，直觉分析一般专注于证实某个假设，并且通常赋予支持某个假设的证据以更大的权重，而非赋予削弱它的证据。理想情况下，情况应当恰恰相反。虽然分析人员通常无法运用科学方法论的统计程序来检验其假设，但他们可以、也应当采纳那种力求驳倒而非证实假设的概念性策略。
    原文

    Scientific method is based on the principle of rejecting hypotheses, while tentatively accepting only those hypotheses that cannot be refuted. Intuitive analysis, by comparison, generally concentrates on confirming a hypothesis and commonly accords more weight to evidence supporting a hypothesis than to evidence that weakens it. Ideally, the reverse would be true. While analysts usually cannot apply the statistical procedures of scientific methodology to test their hypotheses, they can and should adopt the conceptual strategy of seeking to refute rather than confirm hypotheses.

    这个问题有两个方面：人们本性上不会去寻找证伪性证据，而当这类证据被接收到时，它又往往会被打折扣。如果对前一点存有任何疑问，不妨想想人们有多经常通过阅读代表对立观点的报纸和书籍，来检验自己的政治信念和宗教信念。至于后一点，我们已在第2章《感知：为何我们看不见摆在那里等待被看见的东西？》中指出过那种把新信息迁就于既有意象的倾向。如果支持某个假设的信息被当作有效信息接受下来，而削弱它的信息则被判定为可靠性可疑或是无关紧要的反常现象，那么这种做法就很容易实现。当信息以这种方式被处理时，几乎任何一个人们已经相信为真的假设都很容易被“证实”。
    原文

    There are two aspects to this problem: people do not naturally seek disconfirming evidence, and when such evidence is received it tends to be discounted. If there is any question about the former, consider how often people test their political and religious beliefs by reading newspapers and books representing an opposing viewpoint. Concerning the latter, we have noted in Chapter 2, “Perception: Why Can’t We See What Is There to Be Seen?” the tendency to accommodate new information to existing images. This is easy to do if information supporting a hypothesis is accepted as valid, while information that weakens it is judged to be of questionable reliability or an unimportant anomaly. When information is processed in this manner, it is easy to “confirm” almost any hypothesis that one already believes to be true.

    除了寻求证实性证据所涉及的心理陷阱之外，还有一个重要的逻辑问题需要加以考虑。作为否定假设这一科学方法之基础的逻辑推理是：“……某条定律的任何一个证实性实例都不是一个证明性实例，而任何一个否证性实例都是一个证伪性实例。”48 换言之，一个假设永远无法通过列举哪怕大量与该假设相符的证据而得到证明，因为同一批证据也可能与其他假设相符。然而，一个假设却可以通过援引单单一项与之不相容的证据而被驳倒。
    原文

    Apart from the psychological pitfalls involved in seeking confirmatory evidence, an important logical point also needs to be considered. The logical reasoning underlying the scientific method of rejecting hypotheses is that “. . . no confirming instance of a law is a verifying instance, but that any disconfirming instance is a falsifying instance.”48 In other words, a hypothesis can never be proved by the enumeration of even a large body of evidence consistent with that hypothesis, because the same body of evidence may also be consistent with other hypotheses. A hypothesis may be disproved, however, by citing a single item of evidence that is incompatible with it.

    P·C·沃森进行了一系列实验，以检验“人们一般寻求证实性证据而非证伪性证据”这一观点。49 该实验设计所依据的正是上述这一要点：一个假设的成立与否只能通过力求证伪它、而非证实它来加以检验。受试者拿到了三个数字的序列2-4-6，并被要求找出用以生成这一序列的规则。为了做到这一点，他们获准生成自己的三个数字的序列，并向实验者询问这些序列是否符合该规则。他们受到鼓励，可以尽其所愿地生成并询问任意多的序列，并被指示只有在他们相信自己已经找出该规则时才停止。
    原文

    P. C. Wason conducted a series of experiments to test the view that people generally seek confirming rather than disconfirming evidence.49 The experimental design was based on the above point that the validity of a hypothesis can only be tested by seeking to disprove it rather than confirm it. Test subjects were given the three-number sequence, 2 - 4 - 6, and asked to discover the rule employed to generate this sequence. In order to do so, they were permitted to generate three-number sequences of their own and to ask the experimenter whether these conform to the rule. They were encouraged to generate and ask about as many sequences as they wished and were instructed to stop only when they believed they had discovered the rule.

    当然，能够解释序列2-4-6的可能规则有许多。受试者提出了诸如“任何递增的偶数序列”或“任何各数相隔两位的序列”之类的初步假设。正如所预料的那样，受试者一般采取了错误的方法，即力求证实而非排除这类假设。例如，为了检验“规则是任何递增的偶数序列”这一假设，他们可能会问序列8-10-14是否符合该规则。
    原文

    There are, of course, many possible rules that might account for the sequence 2 - 4 - 6. The test subjects formulated tentative hypotheses such as any ascending sequence of even numbers, or any sequence separated by two digits. As expected, the test subjects generally took the incorrect approach of trying to confirm rather than eliminate such hypotheses. To test the hypothesis that the rule was any ascending sequence of even numbers, for example, they might ask if the sequence 8 - 10 - 14 conforms to the rule.

    读到这里、一直跟随着上述推理的读者会认识到，这个假设永远无法通过列举被发现符合所寻求规则的递增偶数序列的例子而得到证明。人们只能通过援引一个递增的奇数序列、并得知它同样也符合该规则，来驳倒这个假设。
    原文

    Readers who have followed the reasoning to this point will recognize that this hypothesis can never be proved by enumerating examples of ascending sequences of even numbers that are found to conform to the sought-for rule. One can only disprove the hypothesis by citing an ascending sequence of odd numbers and learning that this, too, conforms to the rule.

    正确的规则是任何三个递增的数字，无论是奇数还是偶数。由于受试者采取了寻求证实性证据的策略，在沃森的实验中，29名受试者里只有六人在他们第一次认为自己已经找出规则时是正确的。当另一位研究者出于一个略有不同的目的重复这同一个实验时，51名受试者中没有一人在他们第一次认为自己已经找出规则时给出了正确答案。50
    原文

    The correct rule was any three ascending numbers, either odd or even. Because of their strategy of seeking confirming evidence, only six of the 29 test subjects in Wason’s experiment were correct the first time they thought they had discovered the rule. When this same experiment was repeated by a different researcher for a somewhat different purpose, none of the 51 test subjects had the right answer the first time they thought they had discovered the rule.50

    在沃森的实验中，寻求证实性证据而非证伪性证据这一策略之所以特别具有误导性，是因为2-4-6这一序列与如此大量的假设都相符。受试者很容易就能为他们试图证实的几乎任何假设获得证实性证据。重要的是要认识到，与之类似的情形——即证据与好几种不同的假设都相符的情形——在情报分析中极为常见。
    原文

    In the Wason experiment, the strategy of seeking confirming rather than disconfirming evidence was particularly misleading because the 2 - 4 - 6 sequence is consistent with such a large number of hypotheses. It was easy for test subjects to obtain confirmatory evidence for almost any hypothesis they tried to confirm. It is important to recognize that comparable situations, when evidence is consistent with several different hypotheses, are extremely common in intelligence analysis.

    以预警指标清单为例。它们被设计成用来预示一场迫在眉睫的进攻。然而，其中很多指标也与另一种假设相符，即军事调动是为施加外交压力而虚张声势，并不会有军事行动随之而来。当分析人员抓住这些假设中的某一个不放、并去寻找证据来证实它时，他们往往会被引入歧途。
    原文

    Consider lists of early warning indicators, for example. They are designed to be indicative of an impending attack. Very many of them, however, are also consistent with the hypothesis that military movements are a bluff to exert diplomatic pressure and that no military action will be forthcoming. When analysts seize upon only one of these hypotheses and seek evidence to confirm it, they will often be led astray.

    情报分析人员所掌握的证据，在一个重要的意义上不同于那些被要求推断数字序列规则的受试者所掌握的证据。情报分析人员通常处理的问题是：证据与所考虑的假设之间只存在一种概率性的关系。因此，几乎从来无法把任何一个假设完全排除，因为人们至多只能说，鉴于证据的性质某个给定假设是不大可能的，而不能说它是不可能的。
    原文

    The evidence available to the intelligence analyst is in one important sense different from the evidence available to test subjects asked to infer the number sequence rule. The intelligence analyst commonly deals with problems in which the evidence has only a probabilistic relationship to the hypotheses being considered. Thus it is seldom possible to eliminate any hypothesis entirely, because the most one can say is that a given hypothesis is unlikely given the nature of the evidence, not that it is impossible.

    这削弱了从一种旨在排除假设的策略中所能得出的结论，但它绝不能以任何方式为一种旨在证实假设的策略作辩护。
    原文

    This weakens the conclusions that can be drawn from a strategy aimed at eliminating hypotheses, but it does not in any way justify a strategy aimed at confirming them.

    各种情形和数据的不足，往往使得在情报分析中无法应用严格的科学程序——尤其包括用于检验假设的统计方法。然而，寻找相反证据这一基本的概念性策略却当然没有任何理由不能被采用。一种最优的分析策略要求分析人员去搜寻信息以证伪自己所偏爱的理论，而不是采用一种允许接受第一个看来与证据相符的假设的“满意即可”策略。
    原文

    Circumstances and insufficient data often preclude the application of rigorous scientific procedures in intelligence analysis—including, in particular, statistical methods for testing hypotheses. There is, however, certainly no reason why the basic conceptual strategy of looking for contrary evidence cannot be employed. An optimal analytical strategy requires that analysts search for information to disconfirm their favorite theories, not employ a satisficing strategy that permits acceptance of the first hypothesis that seems consistent with the evidence.

    Conclusion

    结论
    关于情报失误，有许多详尽的评估，但关于情报成功的与之相当的描述却很少。在评述有关情报成功的文献时，弗兰克·斯特奇发现了许多成功的例子，但只有三份记述提供了足够的方法论细节，能够揭示那些对成功有所贡献的思维过程和方法。这些记述涉及第二次世界大战期间美国和英国情报工作中的成功努力，即分析德国的宣传、预测德国潜艇的动向，以及估计德国空军未来的能力和意图。51
    原文

    There are many detailed assessments of intelligence failures, but few comparable descriptions of intelligence successes. In reviewing the literature on intelligence successes, Frank Stech found many examples of success but only three accounts that provide sufficient methodological details to shed light on the intellectual processes and methods that contributed to the successes. These dealt with successful American and British intelligence efforts during World War II to analyze German propaganda, predict German submarine movements, and estimate future capabilities and intentions of the German Air Force.51

    斯特奇指出，在这每一项极为成功的努力中，分析人员都采用了这样一些程序，它们“……有助于对关于敌方意图的各种备选假设性估计加以拟定并使其相互对照检验。这三份记述中的每一份都强调了这种让竞争性假设与证据相较量的做法。”52
    原文

    Stech notes that in each of these highly successful efforts, the analysts employed procedures that “. . . facilitated the formulation and testing against each other of alternative hypothetical estimates of enemy intentions. Each of the three accounts stressed this pitting of competing hypotheses against the evidence.”52

    对多个竞争性假设进行同时评估，能够实现一种比分析人员聚焦于单一的、最有可能的解释或估计时所能实现的更为系统、更为客观的分析。对多个竞争性假设进行同时评估，所带来的认知负担远远大于考察单一的、最有可能的假设。把多个假设保持在工作记忆之中，并留意每一项证据如何契合于每一个假设，加起来构成了一项艰巨的认知任务。这就是为什么在对复杂问题的直觉分析中很少采用这种方法。然而，借助第8章《竞争性假设分析》中所描述的一些简单程序，它是能够完成的。
    原文

    The simultaneous evaluation of multiple, competing hypotheses permits a more systematic and objective analysis than is possible when an analyst focuses on a single, most-likely explanation or estimate. The simultaneous evaluation of multiple, competing hypotheses entails far greater cognitive strain than examining a single, most-likely hypothesis. Retaining multiple hypotheses in working memory and noting how each item of evidence fits into each hypothesis add up to a formidable cognitive task. That is why this approach is seldom employed in intuitive analysis of complex issues. It can be accomplished, however, with the help of simple procedures described in Chapter 8, “Analysis of Competing Hypotheses.”

    即便事后回顾，这两个命题仍然似乎有效，这正是为什么沙阿倒台的某些方面仍然令人难以置信。原则上，这些看似有效的理论假定未能对伊朗作出准确估计有三个可能的原因：（1）该理论所假定的一个或多个初始条件实际上并不适用——例如，沙阿并不真的是一个威权统治者。（2）该理论只是部分有效，即存在某些它适用和不适用的特定情况。这些限制条件需要加以明确。（3）该理论基本上有效，但人们不能指望社会科学理论有百分之百的准确性。社会科学不同于自然科学，它处理的是一个概率性的环境。人们无法预见所有可能导致一般规则出现例外的情况，所以所能指望的最好结果，就是给定的条件在大多数时候会导致所规定的结果。
